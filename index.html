Oberoi International School


Campus Life

The OIS auditorium is a state-of the art, well designed theatre with important people frequently coming to talk about important topics.

OIS contains an extremely large field with facilities for multiple sports.



Achievements

Oberoi has co-operated with TED to create TedxYouth@OIS, a program which encourages kids to speak publicly and do TED talks.

The 120 OIS graduates in the class of 2020 have received 648 offers from 231 universities in 12 major countries (US, UK, India, Canada, Italy, France, Hong Kong, UAE, Germany, the Netherlands, Australia, and Grenada), including offers from Oxford, Cambridge, Stanford, and Ashoka. They have been awarded scholarships worth USD 4.84 million.



What's New?

Online School

Online School has been adapted by OIS since the COVID-19 pandemic became a serious issue. The classes have been high-quality and an excellent substitution for regular school.

Return to campus

With the recent downfall of cases, the school is planning to reopen physically, and some 11th and 12th graders are already attending the school.
